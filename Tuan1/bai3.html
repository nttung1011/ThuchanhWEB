One particular book which is recommended reading isThe Street Lawyer by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law frm. Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets, and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawyer is agreat book. It iswell written and interesting. Other books byJohn Grisham include The Firm,The Pelican Brief, andThe Client.
